The class Discord server is where announcements are posted, questions
are answered, and office hours are coordinated. Joining is required.

---

## Step 1: Download Discord

Go to https://discord.com/download and download Discord for your
platform (Windows, macOS, or Linux). Run the installer.

Discord also works in your browser at https://discord.com/app if you
prefer not to install it.

---

## Step 2: Create or log into a Discord account

If you do not already have an account, create a free one at
https://discord.com. Any username is fine.

---

## Step 3: Join the server

Click the following invite link and accept the invite:

**https://discord.gg/JWNpKDFs**

The server will appear in your left sidebar.

---

## Step 4: Change your nickname to your real name

Discord usernames are handles, not names. Set your server nickname
so course staff know who they are talking to.

1. In the left sidebar, right-click the server icon (or tap the
   server name at the top of the channel list).
2. Select **Edit Server Profile**.
3. In the **Nickname** field, type your real first and last name.
4. Click **Save**.

Your nickname is server-specific -- this only changes how you appear
in this server, not on your Discord account globally.

---

## Step 5: Read the rules

Find **#rules** in the channel list and read it before posting
anywhere.

---

## Step 6: Read #staff-intros

Find **#staff-intros** in the channel list. This is where course
staff and duckies have introduced themselves.

Duckies are volunteers (often former students) who help answer
questions during office hours and on Discord. They are not paid
course staff, so be patient and appreciative.

---

## Step 7: Introduce yourself in #student-intros

Find **#student-intros** and send a message introducing yourself.
It can be a sentence or two, or as long as you like. You might include
your name, your major, something you are hoping to get out of the
course, or anything else you want people to know.

---

## Step 8: Get your passphrase

The server onboarding flow provides the passphrase automatically once
you complete the steps it asks. Check your onboarding checklist or
look for a welcome message in the server.

---

## TROUBLESHOOTING

### The invite link says "expired" or "invalid"

Invite links can expire. Contact your instructor for a fresh link.

### I do not see an onboarding flow or welcome message

Some Discord clients show onboarding differently. Look for a checklist
icon near the top of the channel list, or a banner across the top of
the screen when you first join. If you cannot find it, ask in
#student-intros and a staff member or duckie will help.

### Discord is blocked on my network

Try the browser version at https://discord.com/app, or use a mobile
hotspot. Some networks block the Discord desktop app's voice features
but allow browser access.
